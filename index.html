FAQ

Common questions.

Is it really free to start?

Yes. Our free tier is generous and never expires. No credit card needed. You only upgrade when your business grows.

What database does it use?

We use Supabase (Postgres) for the database. Their free tier gives you 500MB storage and handles thousands of rows — more than enough to start.

Can I deploy to Vercel or Netlify?

Absolutely. LaunchKit is built for both. We include a vercel.json and netlify.toml config. Just connect your GitHub repo and it deploys automatically.

Do I need a backend developer?

No. Supabase handles the backend, auth, and database. You just write frontend code and configure the dashboard. No server management needed.
